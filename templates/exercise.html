Exercise & Movement Guide

Simple Exercises

Getting Started with Exercise

Start with these simple exercises that you can do anywhere, anytime. Remember to start slowly and gradually increase intensity.

Desk Stretches
  • Neck rolls (5 each direction)
  • Shoulder shrugs (10 repetitions)
  • Wrist and ankle rotations
  • Seated spinal twist
Perfect for breaking up long periods of sitting
Room Exercises
  • Body-weight squats (10 reps)
  • Wall push-ups (8 reps)
  • Standing leg raises
  • Arm circles (30 seconds)
No equipment needed, suitable for beginners

Walking Meditation

Mindful Walking Practice

Combine the benefits of physical activity with meditation through mindful walking.

Getting Started
  1. Find a quiet path or space
  2. Walk at a natural, comfortable pace
  3. Focus on your breathing
  4. Notice the sensation of walking
